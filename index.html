How we do the research?

some procedures here!

1.We will try to figure out factors related to corruption index.

2.We will model the relationship between corruption index and some variables within countries year by year

3.We will build models through linear regression and use cross-validation to choose the best one.

4.The linear plots for each regression model will be created to clarify the correlation between different variables and the estimated control of corruption.

5.We will use several different types of graphs and tables to illustrate our results and present exploratory data analysis.

6.Histograms and boxplots will be used to show arranged estimated control of corruption of each region from different years.

7.The tendency for each key variable will be presented by correlation and scatter plots.
